list of path commands
- M x y | move pen to (x,y)
- L x y | draw a line to (x,y) from pen pos
- H x | draw a horizontal line of length x
- V y | draw a vertical line of length y
- C x1 y1, x2 y2, xb yb |  first two are control points, b is destination point
- S
- Q x1 y1, xb yb | first is control point, b is destination point
- T
- A
- Z
